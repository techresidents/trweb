FAQ Employers Sign Up

What is Tech Residents?

Technical hiring in its current form is extremely inefficient and painful for both developers and employers, and it's our mission to fix it. We've built a platform that enables you to easily and quickly hire great talent.

Why should I hire using your platform?

Our low-barrier platform attracts talented developers and puts them within your grasp. Our platform will save you time by enabling you to eliminate phone screens from your hiring process and instead listen on demand to developers discuss topics that showcase their technical and cultural fit. You'll also save money compared to hiring recruiters.

How exactly does it work?

Simply sign up and our platform will provide you access to anonymous developer profiles along with the audio from developer chats. If a developer is a potential fit for your organization, you will have the opportunity to extend an interview offer. If a developer accepts your offer, their identity will be revealed so that you may proceed with your hiring process.

How much does it cost?

Our fees are significantly less than traditional recruiters. If you hire a developer within 6 months of extending them an offer through our platform, we will invoice you for the greater of 15% of their annual salary or $12,000. If your hire does not work out and you terminate employment within 90 days, we will issue you a full refund.

Why developer chats?

In our experience as hiring managers, we've found that the best way to determine if a developer is a good fit is through conversation. While it's impractical for you to speak directly with every developer, we believe that our chats are the next best thing. By listening to developer chats you can better evaluate communication skills, technical breadth and depth, and cultural fit within your organization.

Why must developers remain anonymous?

In order to reduce cognitive bias, we think it's best to withhold a developer's identity and background until after your initial evaluation. Additionally, by allowing developers to remain anonymous we're able to keep the barrier to our platform low, and provide you access to more developers.

Do I have to extend an employment offer?

No, there is absolutely no obligation to extend an interview offer at any point in the process. Interview offers made through our platform are non-binding, and only serve as a starting point for further diligence.
