Developers: How it Works

1. Anonymous Profile

Complete your anonymous profile to let employers know about your skills and what you're looking for without revealing your identity.

2. Meaty Topics

Choose a chat topic from our library that truly allows you to showcase your talent. Topics are based on real world challenges and won't be found on any interview checklists.

3. Record Chats

Record your chats alone or with a fellow developer. Some of our chat topics are meaty enough for more than one developer.

4. Highlight Reel

Add recorded chats to your highlight reel to make the audio available to employers.

5. Review Offers

Review interview offers from employers that like what they've heard. There's no obligation to accept an offer.

6. Get Paid

Upon accepting a job offer we'll send you a signing bonus of 20% of our recruitment fee, typically $2,400 - $6,000.
