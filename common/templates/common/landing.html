How it Works for developers

1. Anonymous Profile

Complete your anonymous profile to let employers know about your skills and what you're looking for without revealing your identity.

2. Meaty Topics

Choose a chat topic from our library that truly allows you to showcase your talent. Topics are based on real world challenges and won't be found on any interview checklists.

3. Record Chats

Record your chats alone or with a fellow developer. Some of our chat topics are meaty enough for more than one developer.

4. Highlight Reel

Add recorded chats to your highlight reel to make the audio available to employers.

5. Review Offers

Review interview offers from employers that like what they've heard. There's no obligation to accept an offer.

6. Get Paid

Upon accepting a job offer we'll send you a signing bonus of 20% of our recruitment fee, typically $2,400 - $6,000.

How it Works for employers

1. Create Requisitions

Create requisitions for your open positions in order to track, evaluate, and extend interview offers to developers.

2. Search Talent

Search our pool of talented developers by skillset and job preferences.

3. Listen

Listen to developers discuss meaty topics that showcase their communication skills, technical breadth and depth, and cultural fit.

4. Track & Evaluate

If you like what a developer has to say add them to your applicant tracker so you and the rest of your team can evaluate them.

5. Interview

Extend interview offers to promising developers. If they accept their identity will be revealed so you can move forward with the interview.

6. Hire

There's no obligation to hire, but if you've found a match go ahead and extend a job offer through your usual hiring process.
