FAQ Developers Sign Up

What is Tech Residents?

Technical hiring in its current form is extremely inefficient and painful for both developers and employers, and it's our mission to fix it. We've built a platform that makes it easy and risk-free for developers like you to showcase yourself to hiring companies without having to deal with recruiters, phone screens, and other barriers that get in the way.

Why should I join?

You should join us because:

  • We make it easy and risk-free for you to explore new employment opportunities.
  • You'll save time by taking advantage of our DRY approach to job hunting.
  • You'll maximize your exposure to hiring companies.
  • We'll pay you a signing bonus upon accepting a new job.
  • You'll never have to deal with recruiters again.

Who can join?

Our platform is open to talented software developers of all backgrounds and experience levels, including new grads. We pride ourselves in our ability to recognize talent regardless of the name of your employer or the institution on your diploma.

How exactly does it work?

Simply complete your developer profile and record chats on the topics of your choice. Employers will review your anonymous profile and listen to the audio from your chats to determine if you're a fit for their organization. Employers that like what they hear will extend an interview offer. If you accept the interview and then later accept a job offer we'll pay you a signing bonus.

How much is my signing bonus?

If you accept a job offer through our platform we will pay you a signing bonus of 20% of our recruitment fee as soon as we get paid. For a $100K job this means you will get a $3,000 bonus.

What about privacy?

Employers are only given access to your anonymous developer profile and the audio from your chats. This means that there is no risk of your current employer finding out, or hurting your future chances with other employers. The only time an employer will learn your identity is if you decide to move forward with an interview offer.

Who do I chat with?

You can chat alone or with a friend on a wide variety of real-world software topics. It's best to be as natural as possible in your chats, and some developers find it easier to do this through a real conversation with a fellow developer. In general we recommend completing the shorter chats by yourself, and for the longer chats feel free to go it alone or with a friend.

Why chats?

In our hiring experience, we've found that the best way to determine if a developer is a good fit is through conversation. While it's impractical for you to speak directly will all of our employers, we believe that our chats are the next best thing. By providing employers with the audio from your chats, we can better give them an idea of how you may fit within their organization.

Should I treat chats like an interview?

Absolutely not. The rigidity and formality of an interview all too often only gets in the way. We recommend that you chat like you're speaking with a familiar colleague -- conversational, yet professional, and never rehearsed.

Do I have to accept an interview offer?

No, there's absolutely no obligation to accept an interview offer. Interview offers accepted through our platform are non-binding, and serve as a starting point for further exploration.
