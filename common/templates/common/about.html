About Us

What is Tech Residents?

Tech Residents is a free platform for software developers to talk tech and showcase their talent to hiring companies. We believe that the hiring process in its current form is extremely inefficient and ineffective. Our goal is to bring innovation to the hiring process, making it a better experience for both developers and employers.

Where are you located?

Launched in 2012, Tech Residents is headquartered in Boston, Massachusetts.
