What is it?

Tech Residents is a free platform for software developers to discuss curated technical topics with fellow developers.

The goal of the platform is to streamline and improve the process of hiring software professionals for both developers and companies.

The platform works by recording the technical discussions, making them anonymous for your privacy and protection, and finally making them available to our network of hiring companies.

Who is it for?

Tech Residents is for software professionals who are looking for a new challenge or opportunity in their career. If you're not actively looking for a new position, or are not quite ready to jump into a new opportunity, that's okay too.

Sign up now to discuss technology to improve your skills and network with fellow developers. When the time is right, you can change your preferences to indicate that you're ready to be contacted by our network of hiring companies.

Why join?

Join Tech Residents to showcase your talent and let companies find you. Other reasons to join include:

  • Improve your technical breadth and depth by talking with developers who possess diverse backgrounds and skills.
  • Improve your communication skills.
  • Network with fellow developers.
