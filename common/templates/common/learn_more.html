What is it?

A free platform for software developers to discuss curated technical topics with fellow developers.

Who is it for?

Software professionals of all skill and experience levels who are looking for new career opportunities.

Give Your Resume a Voice

Great companies sift through piles of resumes to find great talent. Don't rely upon your resume to do all the talking.

Recruit friends and colleagues to join you in a conversation, or sign up for a chat with members of the Tech Residents community.

Because Privacy Matters

Anonymized audio recordings (no video) of your chat will be made available to hiring companies. Interested companies will contact you through Tech Residents. Your identity will remain anonymous unless you decide to follow up.

Technical Content

Showcase your talent naturally by discussing real-world technical topics, just like you do every day. Topics are designed to be relevant and thought provoking. Topics do not include programming puzzles, technical-trivia, or brain teasers.

Your Time Is Important

Tired of scheduling another phone interview with yet another company only to never hear back?

Participate in a chat when it's convenient for you. The best part? After the chat, every hiring company in our network will have access to your chat. Only companies that like what they hear will reach out to you, saving everyone time.

You Might Just Learn Something

By participating in technical chats you will be forced to articulate your thoughts improving how you communicate complex ideas with others. By chatting with other members of the Tech Residents community you will be exposed to a myriad of people with different perspectives and expertise.
