Get Started In 3 simple steps

1. Create a Requisition

Requisitions are required to score developers for a position and extend interview offers. You can create a requisition now, or you can come back to this step later.

Create Requisition

2. Search for Talent

While searching for talent you can take notes and score candidates in the context of a job requisition.

For teams, we encourage each member of the team to create an account so they can individually score candidates and cast their vote to make offers.

Search Talent

3. Make an Offer

Once you have found a talented developer and decided to move forward in the hiring process, extend them an interview offer using our tools.
